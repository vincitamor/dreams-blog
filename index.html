Preface

March 17, 2023

If you are like me, then I am sure the simple thought about dreams and its complicated nature excites you. I am getting excited just even writing about it. Dreams are indeed a spectacular phenomenon, one in which has been discussed for centuries due to its unexplainable nature and well deserving of the attention it has receieved. Many known figures throughout history have had theories and beliefs of their own, some contradicting one or the other, but nonetheless all are interesting and credible in their own ways.










You have some weird dream that makes no logical sense, what do you think it symbolizes?

Meaning of Dreams

Dreams are awesome, aren't they? Well, sometimes. Other times they are horrifying.

Where does the story occuring in our dreams come from?

Think about the last time you had an odd dream but there was one
piece of info which you recognized. Well, it is believed that dreams
occur on the foundation of something we know or have seen,
and it creates life for it in the form of our dreams.

< >

WHERE do dreams happen?

The Brain:
Where it all goes down.
While asleep:
This is when dreams occur.

In the cognitive part of your brain,
possibly due to psychical reasoning,
and while you are asleep.

It is important to understand that dreams do not arise from nothing.
They have a purpose, a psychical and subconscious purpose which
cannot be explained, but can be understood by the dreamer if they wish.
I will explain some very interesting info about dreams below.

What causes dreams?

Dream-life is something which has been pondered
and researched but there is no definitive answer, yet.
Some reasons for dreaming can be explained as:

  1.    Dreams can arise due to somatic stimulation while the dream is asleep.
  2.    Dreams can be experiences or thoughts which occurred yesterday, or
       years before which come in the form of an odd and twisted version of a dream.

Where dreams do NOT arise from

Some reasonings may be...:

  • Dreams are not a cause of divine reason.
  • Dreams do not randomly come to the dreamer.
  • Dreams are not from imaginative experiences.
Curious to know how to better remember your dreams?

Before you sleep, make yourself aware that you will dream, and focus on that thought so when you wake up, it will be the first thought which comes to mind. Sleeping at 10 pm and sleeping for 8 hours is also beneficial.
